Melanie Polutta and Bobbi Fox

Who are we?

Melanie Polutta

A cataloging policy specialist at the Library of Congress who works with metadata at the policy level and who *really* wants the power of computers to make her job easier

Bobbi Fox

A software developer who has worked in the Library/Archives domain for more than 15 years

How Did It Start?

The initial call for CodeClub came in late 2014 over the libtechwomen mailing list

https://code4lib.org/conference/2015/sheldon-hess/

Ultimately, there were three groups of 5-6 meeting weekly over "Zoom"

  • Melanie and Bobbi started in a group of 6
  • Initially, we each took a turn finding code written by others to review
